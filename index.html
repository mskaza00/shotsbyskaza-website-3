Behind The Lens

I'm Matthew Skaza, a 17-year-old photographer based in Western Massachusetts. I specialize in sports photography while also capturing portraits and creative moments. With over two years of experience behind the camera, I focus on creating images that preserve meaningful moments.

As a student-athlete and football captain, I bring an athlete's perspective into sports photography by understanding the emotion, preparation, and intensity behind every moment.
